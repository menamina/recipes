Lychee Martini

If you want a cute drink with an edible fruit, Lychee Martini is the way to go

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 ounces vodka
  • 3/4 ounces lychee cordial (such as Giffard's)
  • 3/4 ounces Lustau Manzanilla Papirusa
  • 1 lychee (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Add the vodka, lychee cordial, and sherry into a mixing glass with ice and stir until very cold.
  2. Strain into a chilled Martini glass.
  3. Garnish with lychee prior to serving.
