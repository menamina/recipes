Cosmopolitan

If you're feeling cutesy and want a lighter tasting drink, a Cosmo is for you.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces vodka
  • 1 ounce Cointreau
  • 1 ounce cranberry juice
  • 1 ounce fresh lime juice
  • Ice
  • Orange twist

Steps

  1. Place the vodka, Cointreau, cranberry juice, and lime juice in a cocktail shaker. Add ice and shake until well chilled.
  2. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass, traditionally a coupe or martini glass
  3. Garnish with an orange twist.
